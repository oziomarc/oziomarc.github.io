About Me

Hello and welcome to my website! My name is Ozioma Chukwukeme (but sometimes I go by Ozi). I'm a Nigerian-American sophomore at the New York University Tandon School of Engineering pursuing a major in Computer Science and a minor in Integrated Digital Media. My academic interests include quantum computing, software engineering, and data science. Some of my hobbies include skateboarding, photography, graphic design, and video editing.
